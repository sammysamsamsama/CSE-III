The Problem

Although the internet is widely spread and extremely useful, not everyone has easy access to it.
Whether it be a lack of funds or network coverage, some people cannot simply reach the internet at the press of a button.

Our Solution

We provide a service which allows access to the internet through relatively cheap text messaging.
